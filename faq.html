Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the risk in buying now?
You’ll have 30 days after full launch to request a refund if SnapLayer isn’t right for you. Plenty of time to test — zero pressure.
Why should I buy now instead of waiting for launch?
Early buyers lock in the biggest discounts, receive more starting credits and rollover, get higher referral bonuses, and still have a post‑launch refund window.
Do unused credits roll over?
Normally credits reset monthly. As a prelaunch perk, unused credits roll over up to 5× your monthly limit.
Can I sell or transfer my account?
Yes. You can change your email and fully transfer or resell your license at any time. You may own multiple accounts.
Will I lose my LTD if I don’t use it often?
No. Your lifetime deal never expires. Monthly limits reset each month whether or not you generate images.
Can I upgrade later or stack plans?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or stack additional plans anytime. Agencies can combine limits across accounts.
Can I stack different plans?
Yes. When you merge licenses, the account adopts the highest tier’s features (templates, fields, SnapSheet rows, etc.). Additional lower-tier licenses simply add monthly credits. You can split licenses out later and even transfer or sell them independently.
Can I refer myself for stacking?
Yes. You can use your own referral code to add more licenses. During PreLaunch we automatically apply stacking bonuses so you get the effect of referrer + referee on each stack unit — no need to run separate orders.
How can I trust you and this product?
SnapLayer is part of EmbrFyr. The founder has shipped multiple products and is building SnapLayer in the open. We’re committed to long‑term support.
Can you really build what you’re promising?
Yes. There’s a working prototype today with a clear roadmap (SnapPages, SnapSheet, SnapLinks, PSD import, APIs).
Will SnapLayer disappear like Nexweave?
No. SnapLayer is the image engine for EmbrFyr, not a one‑off tool. Infrastructure and plans ensure continuity.
What’s included in my LTD?
Templates, limits per template, monthly image quota with prelaunch rollover, and storage. Higher tiers add SnapPages, PSD import, API, subaccounts, white‑labeling, etc.
Can I use SnapLayer for client projects?
Yes. Commercial rights are included. Higher tiers include subaccounts to separate client work.
Will there be a public template gallery?
Yes, post‑launch. You’ll browse community templates and optionally share your own for perks.
What makes SnapLayer different?
Speed, simplicity, affordability, CSV bulk generation, referral tools, transparent pricing, and the ability to resell/transfer LTDs.
Will SnapLayer support video or GIF creation?
SnapLayer focuses on static images. Future video personalization may be a separate EmbrFyr product with first access for prelaunch buyers.
Is anything to install?
No. SnapLayer is fully cloud‑based — just log in via your browser.
Integrations and automations?
Planned integrations include Google Sheets, Zapier/Make/Pabbly, and a REST API. Roadmap voting sets priorities.
Custom fonts / branding?
Supported on higher tiers. Upload fonts and white‑label the product to match your brand.
Which image formats can I export?
PNG and JPG at launch. PDF/WebP may follow based on demand. Animated formats aren’t planned.
Is there a cap on total lifetime images?
No lifetime cap. Each plan has a monthly limit (with rollover for prelaunch buyers).
Variables like {{name}}?
Yes. Map text and image fields to CSV columns, query strings, or API parameters for personalization.
Do I need Photoshop?
No. Drag‑and‑drop editing is built in. Flattened PSD import helps with complex designs.
Clone or remix templates?
Yes. Duplicate your templates or remix public ones — ownership stays with you.
When do I get dashboard/beta editor access?
By early August 2025. Core features ready by launch; more integrations roll out per votes.
Early access to new features?
Yes. Prelaunch buyers get first access; outstanding testers may earn bonus credits.
