Referral Program

Earn extra limits by sharing SnapLayer with friends and colleagues. Referrals don’t reduce the price — they boost your plan’s monthly limits by 10% per redemption. Here’s how it works:

Get Your Referral Code

Already purchased a plan? Enter the email you used at checkout to retrieve your referral code for today. A new code is issued each day and can be used by one person.

Redeem a Referral Code

If you have a referral code from another buyer, redeem it here after you’ve purchased your plan. If you paid less than 50% off, we’ll refund the difference to match the 50% maximum discount and increase both accounts’ limits.

Check Your Referral History

Enter your purchase email to see how many times your codes have been used and how much extra capacity you’ve earned. Check back daily for new codes and stats.
