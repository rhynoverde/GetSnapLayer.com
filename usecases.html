Use Cases

Personalization isn’t just for emails. Discover how SnapLayer can power your marketing, outreach and operations with tailor‑made images at scale.

Automated DM & Social Replies

Respond to comments and messages with personalized images that include the commenter’s name, business info or avatar. Great for Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn and more.

Cold Email & Outreach Campaigns

Increase open and reply rates by embedding custom graphics in your outreach emails. Personalize product mockups, stats or review highlights using CSV data.

Proposals, Manuals & Documents

Create on‑brand pitch decks, onboarding manuals, certificates or proposals that automatically adapt to each client’s details. Perfect for agencies and consultants.

Event Badges & Name Tags

Generate hundreds of event badges or name tags in minutes. Use SnapSheet to pull attendee details from a spreadsheet and output print‑ready images.

Social Proof & Review Graphics

Highlight your best testimonials or review stats in eye‑catching images. Automatically insert star ratings, review counts and names from your database.

Performance & Audit Charts

Turn metrics into visuals. SnapLayer can build simple graphs that grow or shrink based on values you supply – ideal for competitor comparisons and progress reports.

Name Tags, Placeholders & Printables

Personalize placards, table tents and event materials with attendee names, titles and images. Export high‑resolution files ready for print.

Onboarding & Certificates

Welcome new hires or celebrate achievements with branded certificates and onboarding materials that populate automatically with names and dates.
